ABOUT

I am a .Net Developer with a primary focus on Back End projects.

I discovered my passion for coding the weird way - I took a job in IT Recruiting and started going to all the meetup events. I realized two things very quickly: I had no idea what anyone around me was saying and I needed to find out. I later enrolled in Eleven Fifty Academy's Software Development program to remedy that.

I built this site as a way to track my progress and provide links to the projects I am most proud off. The portfolio below will continue to grow as I complete projects of greater complexity. If you are interested in seeing my abilities and my approach to problem solving, I reccomend starting there.

If you are interested in a more in depth accounting of my professional career, you can view a copy of my resume here, which is updated monthly.
